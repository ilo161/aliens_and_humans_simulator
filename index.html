How to Play

Civilization needs your help! In 2 minutes the Aliens are coming to your planet. They come every 5,000 years to ensure all is going well.

Click the grass grid.

Select a building to build!

You must build in order.

There are 2 building types: building awards either Community Pts or Production Pts.

Building costs 20 resources.

Aliens do have a preference on building type.
